Jallianwala Bagh

Who can forget the despairing incident which happened at Jallianwala Bagh when general Dyer ordered his army to open fire on hundreds of innocent people talking about the freedom of the country? One of the cruellest acts witnessed during the struggle for independence is certainly the one which happened at this garden in 1919. Located in the vicinity of the Golden Temple, Jallianwala Bagh houses a memorial to honour the innocent people who died during the open fire by a British official. Today, it is one of the most visited places in Amritsar.
